Black Caf Cold Brew
  • A faithful home recreation of the Disneyland Star Wars Land original — bold cold brew, cream cheese vanilla cold foam, Cocoa Puffs on top like asteroids.
⚙ Jedi Brew Protocol · Makes 1
Cold Foam
  • 3 tbsp whipped cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp vanilla syrup
  • 1 tbsp heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp whole milk
The Brew
  • 1½ cups cold brew coffee
  • 1 tbsp vanilla syrup
  • 2 tbsp Cocoa Puffs cereal
  • Ice
Instructions
  1. Combine cream cheese, 2 tbsp vanilla syrup, heavy cream, and milk in a small jar. Froth 1 minute until smooth. Set aside.
  2. Add cold brew to a tall glass. Stir in 1 tbsp vanilla syrup. Top with ice.
  3. Pour cream cheese cold foam over the top.
  4. Sprinkle Cocoa Puffs on top like asteroids. Serve immediately.
Milk and cream should be cold; cream cheese slightly softened. Don't skip the Cocoa Puffs — they are structurally essential to the experience.
